# The Firsts — Dr. Omar Suleiman

> [!info] Series index
> A [Yaqeen Institute](https://yaqeeninstitute.org/series/the-firsts) series by **Dr. Omar Suleiman** on the lives of the companions (Ṣaḥāba) and key figures around the Prophet ﷺ.
> 🎥 [Playlist on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLQ02IYL5pmhHFl7j6wPcFTZmlQvRhsejp) · **185 episodes** (in chronological release order)

Each episode has its own note summarising the companion's life, key moments, and lessons. Episodes are numbered in the series' chronological release order — starting with the introduction, Waraqa ibn Nawfal and Khadijah (ra), through to the most recent.
