# Anatoly Program — Overview

**Plan:** Men Gym Medium + Cardio  
**Level:** INTERMEDIATE  
**Location:** GYM  
**Duration:** 16 weeks  
## Structure

- **2-week microcycle** (Week A / Week B) repeating across the 16 weeks.
- **3 lifting days:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday.
- All other days are **rest** (optional cardio + walking).
- **Warm-up:** 10min — Boost your performance and make your workout safer.
- **Cool-down:** 5min
- Daily target: **10,000–12,000 steps**.
