## Box jumps

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Quads, Glutes, Calves
- **Equipment:** Box

Setup
**Grip:** Arms relaxed at your sides, ready to swing for momentum.
**Position:** Stand facing the box, feet shoulder-width apart, slight bend in the knees, core engaged.
Instructions
- Bend the knees and swing the arms back to load the jump.
- Explosively push through both feet and swing the arms forward to jump onto the box.
- Land softly on both feet simultaneously, with knees bent to absorb the impact.
- Stand fully upright on the box, then step back down one foot at a time.

## Jumping Jacks

- **Primary:** Full body
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Quads, Calves
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Arms relaxed at your sides.
**Position:** Stand with feet together, arms at your sides, core engaged.
Instructions
- Jump both feet out to the sides while simultaneously raising both arms overhead.
- Land softly with feet wider than shoulder-width apart and arms fully raised.
- Jump the feet back together while lowering the arms back down to the sides.

## Mountain Climbers

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Quads, Shoulders, Calves
- **Equipment:** No Equipment, Body Weight

Setup
**Grip:** Hands flat on the floor, shoulder-width apart, fingers pointing forward.
**Position:** Full plank position with body in a straight line from head to heels, core engaged, hips level.
Instructions
- Drive one knee toward the chest in a quick explosive motion.
- Return that foot back to the starting position while simultaneously driving the opposite knee forward.
- Continue alternating legs in a smooth running motion.
- Keep the hips level and avoid letting them rise or sag throughout the movement.
- Maintain a strong core and stable upper body with each rep.

## Push-Up + Superman

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Chest, Lower Back, Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup 
**Grip:** Hands flat on the floor, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, fingers pointing forward
**Position:** Full plank position with body in a straight line from head to heels, core engaged, hips level and spine neutral. 
Instructions 
- Start in a push-up position with hands slightly wider than shoulders. 
- Lower your chest to the floor, perform a push-up.
- At the bottom, transition by lying flat and lifting arms and legs into a superman hold. 
- Hold for a second, lower, and push back up into plank to repeat.

## Running in Place

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Calves, Quads, Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Arms bent at 90°, relaxed at your sides.
**Position:** Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, torso upright.
Instructions
- Lift one knee up to hip height, pushing off the opposite foot.
- Quickly alternate legs in a running motion, landing softly on the balls of the feet.
- Keep the arms bent to control the height of each knee lift.
- Maintain a steady controlled rhythm and avoid landing heavily on the heels.

## Squat + Press

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Quads, Glutes, Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Plate Band

# Setup
Standing with weights at shoulders;
Equipment: Dumbbells or resistance bands
# Insructions
1. Squat down while holding weights at shoulder height.
2. Push through your heels to stand up.
3. Immediately press weights overhead at the top.
4. Lower weights and repeat.

## Vertical jumps

- **Primary:** Full Body
- **Secondary:** Quads, Glutes, Calves
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Clasp hands together in front of the chest or keep arms free at the sides to assist with the jump.
**Position:** Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointed slightly outward, chest up and core engaged.
Instructions
- Bend the knees and push the hips back into a quarter-squat position, swinging arms back for momentum.
- Explosively drive through the full foot, extending hips, knees and ankles to launch off the ground.
- Swing the arms upward forcefully to add height to the jump.
- Land softly on the balls of your feet first, then lower the heels, absorbing the impact by bending the knees.
- Reset to the starting position with control before performing the next rep.
