## Alternate Toe Touches

- **Primary:** Obliques
- **Secondary:** Core, Abs
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** One hand behind the head, elbow wide, other arm extended toward the raised leg.
**Position:** Lie on your back, one leg bent with foot flat on the floor, other leg raised straight up, core engaged.
Instructions
- Lift the shoulder blades off the mat, rotating the upper body toward the raised leg.
- Reach the extended arm up toward the raised foot at the top of the movement.
- Squeeze the abs and hold briefly at the top.
- Lower the upper body back down to the mat slowly and with control.
- Keep the lower back pressed into the mat throughout the movement.

## Bear crawl walkouts

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Quads, Glutes
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip: **Hands flat on the floor, shoulder-width apart.
**Position: **Start standing upright. Feet hip-width apart. Hinge at the hips to place hands on the floor in front of your feet. Spine neutral, core engaged.
Instructions
- From the standing position, hinge forward and place your hands on the floor.
- Walk your hands forward until you reach a strong plank position.
- Keep hips level, core tight, and shoulders stacked over hands.
- Walk your hands back toward your feet while maintaining control.
- Stand up and repeat for the prescribed number of reps.

## Bicycle

- **Primary:** Obliques
- **Secondary:** Core, Abs
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Hands loosely clasped behind the head, elbows wide.
**Position:** Lie on your back with knees bent and lifted off the floor, lower back pressed into the mat, core engaged.
Instructions
- Extend one leg straight out while drawing the opposite knee toward the chest.
- Simultaneously rotate the upper body, bringing the opposite elbow toward the bent knee.
- Switch sides in a smooth pedaling motion, extending the other leg while rotating to the opposite side.
- Keep the lower back pressed into the mat and avoid pulling on the neck.
- Maintain a controlled rhythm throughout — do not rush the movement.

## Body saw

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Abs, Shoulders, Glutes
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup 
**Grip:** Forearms flat on the floor, hands loosely clasped, elbows aligned under shoulders.
**Position:** Forearm plank position with body in a straight line from head to heels, core engaged, hips level.
Instructions 
- Start in a solid forearm plank position with feet on sliders or a smooth surface. 
- Brace your core and slowly shift your body backward by pushing through your forearms, sliding your feet behind you. 
- Then pull your body forward, returning to the starting plank position. 
- Keep your hips level and your core engaged throughout the movement.

## Climbers

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Abs, Quads
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Hands flat on the floor, shoulder-width apart, fingers pointing forward.
**Position:** Full plank position with body in a straight line from head to heels, core engaged, hips level.
Instructions
- Drive one knee toward the chest in a quick explosive motion.
- Return that foot back to the starting position while simultaneously driving the opposite knee forward.
- Continue alternating legs in a smooth running motion.
- Keep the hips level and avoid letting them rise or sag throughout the movement.
- Maintain a strong core and stable upper body with each rep.

## Dolphin

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Lower Back
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup
**Grip:** Forearms flat on the floor, hands loosely clasped, elbows aligned under shoulders.
**Position:** Forearm plank position with body in a straight line, core engaged and spine neutral.
Instructions
- Press through the forearms and lift the hips up and back, forming an inverted V-shape with the body.

- Keep the arms firmly pressed into the floor and avoid collapsing in the shoulders throughout the movement.

## Hanging 90-Degree Leg Scissor Kicks

- **Primary:** Abs
- **Secondary:** Obliques, Core, Forearms
- **Equipment:** Pull Up Bar

Setup
**Grip:** Hold the bar with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
**Position:** Hang from the pull-up bar with arms fully extended, core engaged, body straight and stable.
Instructions

- From this position, alternately raise each leg as high as possible in a controlled scissor motion.
- Keep the core tightly engaged throughout to avoid swinging the body.

## Plank

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Glutes, Quads
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

**Setup**
**Grip:** Place your forearms flat on the floor, elbows directly below your shoulders, hands relaxed and pointing forward.
**Position:** Extend both legs straight back, resting on your toes. Form a straight line from head to heels — hips level, core braced, and gaze directed toward the floor.
**Instructions**
- Squeeze your core, glutes, and quads to maintain a rigid, straight body position.
- Keep your hips level — avoid letting them sag down or rise up.
- Breathe steadily and hold the position for the prescribed amount of time.

## Plank side steps

Setup

## Walking plank

- **Primary:** Core
- **Secondary:** Shoulders, Chest, Glutes
- **Equipment:** Body Weight, No Equipment

Setup 
**Grip:** Place both hands flat on the floor, directly below your shoulders, fingers pointing forward.
**Position:** Start in a high plank position with arms fully extended, body forming a straight line from head to heels. Core braced, hips level, feet hip-width apart.
Instructions
- Step one hand forward, then the other, walking your hands out in front of you.
- Walk your feet forward to follow, returning to the plank position.
- Keep your hips level throughout — avoid rocking side to side.
- Maintain a tight core and neutral spine during the entire movement.
- Continue walking for the prescribed distance or number of repetitions.

## Weighted Russian Twist

- **Primary:** Obliques
- **Secondary:** Abs
- **Equipment:** Plate

Setup
**Grip:** Extend the arms in front of the chest and hold a weight plate with both hands
**Position:** Sit on the mat with knees bent, feet flat on the floor, torso leaned back at a 45°. Anchor the feet under dumbbells to keep them stationary
Instructions
- Rotate the torso to one side, bringing the weight toward the floor beside the hip.
- Return to the center and rotate to the opposite side in a controlled motion.
- Keep the arms extended and the weight at chest level throughout the rotation.
- Keep the chest up and avoid rounding the lower back during the movement.
