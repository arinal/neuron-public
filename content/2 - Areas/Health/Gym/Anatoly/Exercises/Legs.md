## Abductor Machine

![Abductor Machine](img/abductor-machine.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** —
- **Equipment:** Abduction Machine

Setup
**Grip:** Hold the side handles lightly with both hands for stability.
**Position:** Sit upright on the machine, back flat against the pad, knees bent at 90°, legs pressed against the outer pads.
Instructions
- Push both legs outward against the pads.
- Open as far as the range of motion allows, squeezing the outer glutes and hip abductors at the end.
- Hold briefly at the widest point.
- Slowly return the legs back to the starting position with control.

## Banded hip thrust

![Banded hip thrust](img/banded-hip-thrust.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** Hamstrings, Core
- **Equipment:** Long Band

- Lower the hips back down with control.

## Banded side steps

## Barbell walking lunges

![Barbell walking lunges](img/barbell-walking-lunges.jpg)

- **Primary:** Quads
- **Secondary:** Glutes, Hamstrings, Calves
- **Equipment:** Barbell

Setup
**Grip:** Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, palms facing forward. Place it across the upper traps, keeping elbows pointing down and back to create a stable shelf.
**Position:** Stand tall with feet hip-width apart, bar resting on the upper traps. Core braced, chest up and gaze forward.
Instructions
- Step one foot forward and lower your body by bending both knees until the back knee nearly touches the floor.
- Keep the front knee tracking directly over the toes and the torso upright throughout the descent.
- Drive through the heel of the front foot to rise up and bring the back foot forward to meet the front.
- Step immediately forward with the opposite foot and repeat the lunge on the other side.

## Bulgarian-style deadlift

![Bulgarian-style deadlift](img/bulgarian-style-deadlift.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** Hamstrings, Lower Back, Core
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell, Bench

**Setup**
**Grip:** Hold a dumbbell in the working hand with a neutral grip, arm hanging straight down.
**Position:** Place one knee on the bench behind you and step the opposite foot forward on the floor. Hinge slightly at the hip, keeping your back flat, chest up, and core engaged. The dumbbell hangs at arm's length beside the front leg.
**Instructions**
- Drive through the heel of the front foot, extending your hip to lift the dumbbell and stand upright.
- Keep the dumbbell close to your leg throughout the movement.
- Squeeze your glutes at the top and fully extend your hip.
- Slowly hinge back at the hip, lowering the dumbbell to the starting position in a controlled motion.
- Repeat for the prescribed number of repetitions, then switch sides.

## Cable Hip Abduction

![Cable Hip Abduction](img/cable-hip-abduction.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** Quads, Core
- **Equipment:** DAP Machine (Cable)

**Setup**
**Grip:** Hold onto a stable support (cable machine frame or upright) with both hands for balance.
**Position:** Attach the ankle cuff to the working leg and set the cable pulley to the lowest position. Stand sideways to the machine, with the working leg closest to it. Keep your standing leg slightly bent and torso slightly hinged forward.
**Instructions**
- Lift the working leg out to the side in a wide arc, keeping it straight.
- Raise as high as comfortable while keeping your hips square and level.
- Pause briefly at the top, squeezing the glute of the working leg.
- Slowly lower the leg back to the starting position in a controlled motion.
- Avoid leaning your torso to the opposite side — keep the movement isolated to the hip.

## Classic deadlift

![Classic deadlift](img/classic-deadlift.jpg)

- **Primary:** Hamstrings
- **Secondary:** Glutes, Lower Back, Forearms
- **Equipment:** Barbell

**Setup**
**Grip:** Grip the bar just outside the legs with both hands using a double overhand or mixed grip. 
**Position:** Stand with feet hip-width apart, bar over the mid-foot. Hinge at the hips and bend the knees until the shins touch the bar. Chest up, shoulders directly over or slightly in front of the bar, core braced.
**Instructions**
- Take a deep breath, brace the core hard and create full-body tension before initiating the pull.
- Press the floor away through the full foot, extending the knees and hips simultaneously.
- Keep the bar in contact with the legs throughout the entire lift, maintaining a vertical bar path.
- Lock out by fully extending the hips and squeezing the glutes at the top, standing tall.

## Wide-stance deadlift (barbell or smith machine)

![Wide-stance deadlift (barbell or smith machine)](img/wide-stance-deadlift-barbell-or-smith-machine.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** Lower back, Biceps, Hamstrings
- **Equipment:** Smith Machine

**Setup**
**Grip:** Hold the bar with an overhand grip, hands shoulder-width apart, arms hanging straight down inside your legs.
**Position:** Stand with feet wider than shoulder-width, toes slightly turned out. Hinge at the hips and bend the knees to reach the bar. Keep your back flat, chest up, and core engaged.
**Instructions**
- Drive through both heels to lift the bar, extending your hips and knees simultaneously.
- Keep the bar close to your body as it travels upward.
- Stand fully upright, squeezing your glutes at the top.
- Hinge back at the hips and bend the knees to lower the bar in a controlled motion.
- Maintain a flat back throughout — avoid rounding your lower spine at any point.

## ​Glute-Focused Hyperextension

![​Glute-Focused Hyperextension](img/glute-focused-hyperextension.jpg)

- **Primary:** Glutes
- **Secondary:** Hamstrings, Lower Back
- **Equipment:** Hyperextension Bench

Setup
**Grip:** Hands loosely clasped in front of you.
**Position:** Position yourself on the hyperextension bench with hips resting on the pad, feet secured under the footrests, torso hanging down.
Instructions
- Press the hips into the pad and raise the torso upward, squeezing the glutes.
- Rise until the body forms a straight line from head to heels.
- Squeeze the glutes firmly at the top and hold briefly.
- Lower the torso back down slowly and with control.
- Keep the movement initiated from the glutes rather than the lower back throughout.

## ​Squats

![​Squats](img/squats.jpg)

- **Primary:** Quads
- **Secondary:** Glutes, Hamstrings, Lower Back
- **Equipment:** Barbell

Setup
**Grip:** Hold the barbell with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, bar resting across the upper traps.
**Position:** Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly turned out.
Instructions
- Lower into a squat by pushing the hips back and bending the knees until thighs are parallel to the floor.
- Keep the knees tracking over the toes and the chest up throughout the descent.
- Keep the heels flat on the floor and weight distributed through the mid-foot.
- Drive through the heels to return to the standing position.
