# Workout — Week A (plan week 28)

Training days: **Mon / Wed / Fri**. Each session: 10 min warm-up → work → 5 min cool-down.

> Part of a 2-week cycle. See [[01-Program-Overview]].

## Monday — Legs
_6 exercises_

- **Low-bar squat** — 1 set × 3 _(rest 60s; Single set)_
- **Leg press (Сlassic stance)** — 4 sets × 12/12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Goblet squat** — 4 sets × 12/12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Dumbbell lunges** — 3 sets × 12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Leg extensions** — 3 sets × 12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Leg curls** — 3 sets × 12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_

## Wednesday — Chest / Shoulders
_6 exercises_

- **Bench press** — 1 set × 3 _(rest 60s; Single set)_
- **Alternating incline dumbbell press** — 4 sets × 8/8/8/8 _(rest 60s)_
- **Dips** — 4 sets × 6/6/6/6 _(rest 60s)_
- **Seated dumbbell press** — 3 sets × 10/10/10 _(rest 60s)_
- **Cable chest fly** — 3 sets × 12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Push-ups** — 2 sets × Max/Max _(rest 60s)_

## Friday — Back / Grip / Triceps
_7 exercises_

- **Classic deadlift** — 1 set × 3 _(rest 60s; Single set)_
- **Seated row (horizontal row machine)** — 4 sets × 8/8/8/8 _(rest 60s)_
- **Classic Pull-Ups** — 4 sets × 12/12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Bent-over rows with dumbbells** — 4 sets × 12/12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Dumbbell pullover** — 3 sets × 12/12/12 _(rest 60s)_
- **Hammer curls** — 3 sets × 6/6/6 _(rest 60s)_
- **Plate holds standing** — 3 sets × Maxs/Maxs/Maxs _(rest 60s)_
